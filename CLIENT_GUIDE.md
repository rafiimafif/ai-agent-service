# AgentFlow: Client Portal Guide

Welcome to **AgentFlow**! 

AgentFlow is a futuristic, AI-powered service agency platform. Unlike traditional agencies, we utilize a hybrid team of expert human project managers and specialized AI Agents (like Research bots, Coding bots, and Automation bots) to deliver high-quality work for your projects at lightning speed.

This guide will walk you through how to use the Client Portal to request services, track your project's progress, and manage your billing.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal

- **Login Page:** `/login`

---

## 2. The Client Dashboard (`/portal`)

Your dashboard is your central hub for all interactions with our agency. Here you will see:
- A high-level overview of all your active and past projects.
- The overall completion percentage of each project.
- Key financial information like your estimated budget and assigned AI tasks.

If you don't have any active projects yet, you'll see a prompt inviting you to submit your first project brief!

---

## 3. Submitting a New Project Brief

To request new work, you need to submit a project brief.

1. Click the **"+ Submit New Brief"** button in the top right corner of your dashboard.
2. You will be taken to a multi-step form:
   - **Step 1 (Overview):** Enter the project title, a brief description of your goals, and your estimated budget.
   - **Step 2 (Details):** List your specific requirements and what exactly you expect us to deliver (e.g., "Figma UI designs", "Next.js Codebase"). You can also add specific data sources if needed.
   - **Step 3 (Review):** Review the details of your brief and click **"Submit Brief 🚀"**.
3. Once submitted, our team will review the brief. Upon acceptance, the project will be officially kicked off and AI Agents will be assigned to workstreams.

---

## 4. Tracking Project Progress

Once a project is active, click on the project card in your dashboard to view the **Project Detail Page**.

This page provides full transparency into how our AI Agents are working on your request:
- **Overall Progress:** A progress bar showing the total completion percentage of the project.
- **AI Agent Workstream:** A real-time timeline showing exactly which AI agent is working on what task. 
  - You will see tasks marked as **"Working"** (with a pulsing indicator) or **"Done"**.
  - This allows you to watch the step-by-step execution of your project in real-time, whether it's a Research agent analyzing competitors or a Coding agent scaffolding a codebase.
- **Project Details:** A summary of your budget, submission date, and brief status.

---

## 5. Viewing Invoices

Billing is fully integrated into the platform for your convenience.

On the **Project Detail Page**, look at the right-hand column under the **"Invoices"** section:
- Here you will see a list of all invoices issued for the project (e.g., initial deposit, final milestone).
- You can track whether an invoice is **"SENT"**, **"PAID"**, or **"OVERDUE"**.
- *(Admin feature note: Our admins can generate downloadable PDF invoices for your accounting department).*
